Our Story

Born from Passion, Built on Trust

In 1985, our founder started Ambika Sweet with a small rented space and an unshakeable belief: every Indian deserves sweets made purely, honestly, and with love. His first product — plain barfi — sold out on day one.

Word spread quickly across Pune's Katraj neighborhood. Within a year, Ambika Sweet had a loyal following. By 1995 we expanded to a full-fledged shop with a display kitchen. Today we're a Pune institution — trusted by families across generations.

Our team continues the legacy — blending traditional recipes with modern hygiene and packaging standards — while keeping every sweet as authentic as it was on day one.

Our Core Values

🌿

Pure Ingredients

We source fresh milk daily, use pure desi cow ghee, and handpick dry fruits. No preservatives, no artificial flavors — ever.

🧼

Hygiene First

Our FSSAI-certified kitchen undergoes daily deep cleaning. All staff wear PPE, and we maintain strict cold-chain for dairy products.

📜

Traditional Recipes

Our recipes have been perfected over 3 generations. We never take shortcuts with cooking time, ratios, or methods.
